Dream of the Endless

Dream of the Endless, also called Morpheus, rules the Dreaming — the realm of dreams and nightmares. Mysterious and powerful, he shapes the stories of mortals while struggling with duty and change.

Companions in his realm

Luciene is the librarian and steward of the Dream world.

Mathew is the messanger and Companion of lord Morpheus when he is travelling.

Pumpkin, he is a builder and servent who looks after lands fields of the Dream world.

Abel and Cain, they two are lovely brothers who is one of the trusted companion of Lord Morpheus.
